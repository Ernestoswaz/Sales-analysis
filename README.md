# Sales Performance Analysis

## Problem
The business needs to understand sales performance across products, regions, and time in order
to identify revenue drivers and underperforming areas.

## Dataset
Sales transaction data containing order date, product, region, units sold, and sales amount.

## Approach
- Cleaned and validated sales data
- Calculated key KPIs such as Total Sales, Average Sales per Unit, and Monthly Growth
- Applied Pareto (80/20) analysis to identify top-performing products
- Built interactive dashboards in Power BI for visualization

## Key Insights
- Approximately 20% of products contribute nearly 80% of total revenue
- Sales showed a decline during 3rd quarter (July - Septmber), compared to other quarters
- Certain regions consistently outperformed others in average sales per unit

## Tools Used
- SQL
- Power BI
- Google Sheets

## Outcome
The analysis helps stakeholders focus on high-impact products and regions, improving decision-making and sales strategy.
